IELTS Tips & Tricks

Quick, proven moves you can put to work today.

Listening: map questions Use compass words (north/south/east/west) and landmarks; track the speaker’s direction with your finger.
Reading: True/False/Not Given Not Given means missing info. Do not infer. If a detail is not explicitly supported, it is NG.
Reading: timing Around 18–20 minutes per passage. Skim titles and topic sentences before diving into questions.
Writing Task 2 Plan for 4–5 minutes. Clear stance in the thesis + two focused body paragraphs.
Writing Task 1 (Academic) Overview first: major trends and extremes. Group data rather than listing every tiny change.
Speaking Part 2 Template: opening line → 2–3 subpoints → short wrap. Use signposts like “Firstly, Next, Finally”.
